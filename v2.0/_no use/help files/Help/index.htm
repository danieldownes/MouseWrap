Mouse Wrap 2.04

 

Mouse Wrap is a small utility to enhance the mobility of the Windows mouse pointer.
It does this by wrapping the pointer around the screen. If the pointer is moved to any edge of the screen, the pointer will appear at the opposite side of the screen; See the graphical demo (below) for elaboration.

It may take time to get used to the way in which you can now use your mouse, but once you realise how much quicker it is to get to things at the other side of the screen, you'll wonder how you managed without!


A new feature in Mouse Wrap 2 is Movement Pattern Recognition. Once a distinct movement is made, a custom action is executed. Currently the available actions are: Cut, Copy, Paste, Forward and Backward navigation in Windows Explorer and Internet Explorer.
